fix(ci): correct pnpm peerDependencyRules allowedVersions syntax

The allowedVersions field in pnpm-workspace.yaml expects a flat map of
"dependency-name: version", not a nested object specifying the dependent
package first.

The previous syntax (tailwindcss-animate: { tailwindcss: "*" }) caused
pnpm v11 to crash during lockfile resolution on fresh installs with:
[ERR_PNPM_INVALID_ALLOWED_VERSION_SELECTOR] bareSpecifier.startsWith is not a function

Corrected to tailwindcss: "*".
